About WALEXX

The Waje Loyalty Exchange (WALEXX) Platform is a centralized platform that unifies loyalties from different loyalty-offering-service, thereby making it possible for a loyalty earner to redeem loyalty points (either from an individual loyalty program or an aggregate of different loyalty points) from any loyalty program on the platform.
